How to Use
  1. Input Text: Paste your reading assignment into the main box, or click "Upload Document".
  2. Format: Click "Format Text" to prepare the document for reading.
  3. Customize: Use the toggles above to change colors, font, and enable the Line Focus tool.
  4. Read: Hover your mouse over the text to highlight individual sentences and maintain focus.
